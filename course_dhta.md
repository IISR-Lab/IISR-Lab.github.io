## ğŸ« Course Philosophy

### "From Tool to Partner"
In the past, Science and Humanities were parallel lines. Today, AI is breaking this wall. We believe that **Domain Experts (Humanities)** must become the **Architects of AI**.

<div class="quote-box">
    "In this concerto, Science provides the 'methods' and 'tools', while Humanities endows innovation with 'direction', 'value', and 'warmth'." â€” Prof. Richard Tzong-Han Tsai
</div>

<div class="highlight-box">
    <h3 style="margin-top: 0; color: #2c3e50;">ğŸš€ The Technical Evolution</h3>
    <p>Our curriculum evolves with the SOTA technology to ensure students learn the most relevant skills:</p>
    <ul style="line-height: 1.6;">
        <li><b>2025 Focus: RAG & Agentic Workflows</b><br>
        Moving beyond fine-tuning. We focus on <b>Long Context Prompting</b>, <b>Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)</b>, and designing <b>Agent SOPs</b> to solve complex logic tasks without catastrophic forgetting.</li>
        <li><b>2024 Focus: Instruction Fine-Tuning (SFT)</b><br>
        Teaching students how to curate datasets to fine-tune open-source models (like Llama 3 / TAIDE) for specific tasks, emphasizing that "Data Quality is Key."</li>
    </ul>
</div>
